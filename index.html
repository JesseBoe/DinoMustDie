Dino Must Die
How To Play:
Controls:
Action
Move Arrow Keys Dpad/Joystick
Jump Spacebar 'X' Button
Reset R Key Left Bumper
Get Ability Up Arrow Up Dpad/Joystick
Use Star S Key 'Square' Button
Star Jump:

To perform a star jump, you first must first collect the powerup. Pressing and holding the 'S' key will create a star. Once you release the key, the star will be thrown in a direction depending on if you are pressing up, down or are neutral. The easiest way I find is to first jump into the air and then throw the star down. This should cause the star to bounce into you allowing you to reach new hieghts and avoid spikes!
